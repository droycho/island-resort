Things to do while you're here

  • Kayak

    • Kayaking is a popular pasttime for many locals and tourists alike. There are numerous rental sites along the beach with two located in the main lobby of the resort.

  • Surf

    • Like kayaking, surfing is a very popular sport for everyone on the island. Our resort proudly hosts an annual surfing competition in the Winter season. Bookings go fast during this time, so plan accordingly!

  • Camping

    • We don't recommend camping. 500 people were mauled by bears last year alone. Only 600 visited the island that year. Don't camp.

  • Build a sand castle

    • Building sandcastles is for cumps. We build sand-palaces because we're better than you and your whole family.
